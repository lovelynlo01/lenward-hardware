Our story

Three decades behind the counter

Lenward Hardware Trading started as a small corner shop selling nails and rope, and grew into a full hardware store stocking paint, tools, plumbing parts, and light machines. We've been serving Kota Kinabalu since 1994, and we still believe the best advice comes from someone who's actually used the tool.

This website is here so you can see what we carry before you make the trip down — but for fitting, color matching, and anything that needs a closer look, we'll always ask you to come by the shop.
